Make Human

Description

A Human is one of the most dominant species on the Earth, let's go over what a human is made up of.

Ingredients(by Mass)

  1. 65% Oxygen
  2. 18% Carbon
  3. 10% Hydrogen
  4. 3% Nitrogen
  5. 1.4% Calcium
  6. 1.1% Phosphorous
  7. 1% Potassium
  8. ~0.5% Combination of Sulfur, Sodium, Chlorine, and Magnesium

Directions

  • A head
  • 1 Brain(optional)
  • 2 Ears
  • 2 Eyes
  • 1 Mouth
  • Torso
  • 2 Legs
  • 2 Arms
